03:00 PM - 06:00 PM

Line Follower.

For Electronics Guys

Rules:

  1. Maximum of four (4) persons per team (Cross years and Cross Branches allowed).
  2. The dimensions of the vehicle must not be more than 15 x 15 x 15 cm.
  3. No LEGO, EV3 or any pre made kits allowed.
  4. Controllers allowed: Arduino, Raspberry Pi.
  5. The width of the black strip would be 3 cm.
  6. The judging committee possess the rights to disqualify any team on any grounds.
  7. Track will be revealed on the day of the event.
  8. Marking scheme will be disclosed on spot.
  9. Each team will be given two trials before the main run for any changes necessary.
  10. Exciting Awards would be disclosed on spot.
